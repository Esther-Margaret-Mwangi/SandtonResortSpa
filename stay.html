******CHOOSE YOUR LUXURIOUS ROOM******

All our rooms are spacious and comfortable offering lovely sweeping views of the
surrounding hills and lush tropical gardens. Our rooms feature
mordern amenities such as flat-screen TVs, free Wi-Fi and plush bedding.
Whether you are here for business or pleasure, our rooms are the perfect
place to unwind after a long day.





DELUXE EXECUTIVE ROOMS





The Deluxe Executive Room is fitted with 1 Double bed and comprises a toilet
and shower, wardrobe/closet, terrace/garden and a flat-screen TV. Room services are offered.
You also get a personal chef to cook your meals.





HONEYMOON SUITE


Honeymoons at Sandton are memorable because we take our time
to pamper the couple. Meals for two are offered on full board basis in
our luxurious one bedroomed suites. You and your loved one are bound to fall in love
again as you arrive to a bubbly welcome, as well as the chance to enjoy a
private dinner on the dining room balcony, plus a memorable boat ride.





FOUNTAIN EXECUTIVE ROOMS





Fountain Executive Rooms offer the ultimate flexibility in guest accommodation.
They are available as single rooms or self-contained 2 and 3 Bedroomed suites with access to a
luxurious lounge, a fully equipped kitchen, and an extra TV in the living room.





VILLA ROOMS





The Villa Rooms offer the ultimate flexibility in guest accommodation.
Located amidst richly landscaped gardens overflowing their banks, these rooms also
feature a luxurious lounge, a fully equipped kitchen and an extra TV in the living room.
