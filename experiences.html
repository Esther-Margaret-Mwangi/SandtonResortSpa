SWIMMING




There is nothing better than enjoying a swim in our supersized pool while enjoying
the lovely view of the sunset. Swimming is free of charge every time you stay with us.






GOLFING




Enjoy golfing in our beautiful fields.






BIKING




If you love cycling you will enjoy biking at Sandton. No need to
bring your bike. We have some on site for hire and lovely paved routes with interesting
flora and fauna to identify along the way. Ride alone or with your young ones. Whatever you choose we guarantee
you will enjoy the ride.






BOAT SAFARIS




There is no better way to see the famous Burton flamingos than on
a boat Safari. With daily departures right from our lakefront, don’t miss your chance to
witness these amazing birds in their natural habitat.






HORSE RIDING




Enjoy horse riding in Sandton. A proffesional rider will guide you
through this experience. Enquire at the reception for the charges.
