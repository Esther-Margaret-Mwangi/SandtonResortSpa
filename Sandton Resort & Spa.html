ABOUT US




Sandton Resort & Spa was opened in December 2002 and named after the owner Mr.Sandton Leleu.
It is located in Mayland by the shores of Lake Burton in Dubai.The resort's location was selected because
of the beautiful view of the lake, the calm and quiet environment far away from the town center hence providing
the serene environment suitable for relaxing and escaping the city's noise.

Sandton offers high standards accommodation and modern sophistication on a budget.
This ensures that you get pure value for your money and gives you a sense of being home away from home.
Our dedicated and very informative staff will make you feel well attended whenever needed.
