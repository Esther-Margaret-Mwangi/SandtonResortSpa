******REDISCOVER WELLNESS******

Whether you have time for a whole day of rejuvenation or for few hours
to refresh, Siara's holistic section of spa and sauna experiences amidst sensational
surroundings promise complete well-being and serenity.





HOT STONE MASSAGE




The heat from warmed volcanic stones combines with therapeutic
oils ease tense muscles and damaged soft tissues throughout the body, leaving
you relaxed and rejuvenated.





FACIAL THERAPY




Take a moment to indulge in a facial treatment tailored
to your skin type. Select from a choice of experiences that will deeply cleanse,
exfoliate and nourish your skin.





EXFOLIATE GLOW




Refresh, revive and reveal healthy glowing skin using our
natural selection of exfoliants to help stimulate blood circulation and body
detoxification.





SAUNA



Enjoy a lovely experience of dry or wet heat sessions in Siara's sauna.





SIARA SALON & BARBER SHOP




Look good with the assistance of our talented hairdressers,
barbers and beauticians at the fully kitted Salon and Barbershop. Highly trained
in hairdressing and grooming, the professional salon and barbershop
artisans will leave you looking your very best whether you chose the service as a
compliment to the restoration and rejuvenation provided at the Spa,
for a glam-up session before a big event or as a quick fix-me-up at any time of day.
